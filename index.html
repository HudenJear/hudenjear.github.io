During my graduate and work period, my researches are mainly about:

1. IQA: image quality assessment

Image quality plays a crucial role in medical record quality control. Therefore, we should develop an image quality assessment algorithm specifically for medical images.

2. Cataract image restoration

Seeing through the cloudy lenses of cataract patients, ophthalmologists can diagnose whether these patients have other highly blinding eye diseases. Consequently, an image restoration method was specifically developed for cataract images.

3. RNA optimization

The mysterious RNA sequences hold significant potential for various applications. RNA vaccines and industrial protein manufacturing processes can leverage RNA optimization techniques to achieve higher protein expression rates. In my research, I pioneered a novel RNA optimization method that combines deep learning with unique loss functions, resulting in a multi-factor optimized mRNA coding sequence (CDS).
